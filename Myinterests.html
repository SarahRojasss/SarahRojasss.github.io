OVERVIEW

My main interests lie withing watching movies or tv shows, listening to songs, and, what is arguably my largest interest, reading.

MOVIES AND TV-SHOWS

I enjoy watching movies and tv-shows, especially with family, because I am hugely absorbed in stories and the development that goes along with them. I also enjoy guessing what will happen in the movies, and it makes me unnecessarily ecstatic when I end up being correct about a certain outcome.

SONGS

Similarly to movies, songs allow me to experience human emotions in a quicker span of time. For example, when I listen to Taylor Swift, by paying attention to her lyrics, I feel emerged into her story and it makes me feel as though I have gone through them myself. These can be expressed as heartbreak, friendship bonds, and love songs.

READING

Lastly, but most importantly, reading is more special to me than any other activities I partake in because I ache for that feeling of entertainment, drama, and suspense, Which, although is also available through movies, seems more realistic when reading as I get to imagine what everything looks like.
